Product Details

Our Starbucks Reserve® Cold Brew—a nuanced, smooth cup of coffee, perfect over ice—is extraordinarily uplifting in a bold form.

Dietary Restrictions

Caffeine Sensitivity: Cold Brew contains caffeine, which may not be suitable for individuals who are sensitive to caffeine or those who are advised by their doctors to limit or avoid caffeine.
Pregnancy and Breastfeeding: Caffeine in Cold Brew might not be safe during pregnancy or breastfeeding.
Diabetes: The sugar in the Cold Brew can affect blood sugar levels, so it may not be suitable for people with diabetes.
High Blood Pressure: The caffeine in Cold Brew may affect blood pressure, so it should be limited in people with high blood pressure.
Fluid-Restriction Diet: Cold Brew is a liquid and should be counted towards daily fluid intake for those on a fluid-restriction diet.
