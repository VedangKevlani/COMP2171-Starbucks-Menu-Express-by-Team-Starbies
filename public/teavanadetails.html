Black Tea Latte

Product Details

Pure black tea is steamed with milk then sweetened to create this smooth, creamy latte. A signature favourite of tea lovers.

Dietary Restrictions

Gluten Intolerance/Celiac Disease: All the ingredients in the Black Tea Latte are naturally gluten-free. However, cross-contamination can occur, so it is always best to check the labels or ask the manufacturer if you are unsure.
Diabetes: The sugar in the drink can affect blood sugar levels, so it may not be suitable for people with diabetes.
Vegan Diet: The recipe includes milk, which is not suitable for vegans. However, you can substitute this with plant-based alternatives.
Lactose Intolerance: People with lactose intolerance should be cautious as this drink contains milk, which contains lactose.
High Blood Pressure: While the recipe does not contain salt, the high sugar content may not be suitable for people with high blood pressure.
