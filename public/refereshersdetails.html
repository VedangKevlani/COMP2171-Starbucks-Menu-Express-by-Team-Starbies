Product Details

Sweet strawberry flavors accented by passion fruit and açaí notes, shaken with ice and real strawberry pieces—a welcoming sweet spot of refreshment.

Dietary Restrictions

Gluten Intolerance/Celiac Disease: The ingredients in Strawberry Acai are naturally gluten-free. But, cross-contamination could occur, so always check labels or ask the manufacturer.
Diabetes: Strawberry Acai contains sugars, which can affect blood sugar levels. Therefore, it may not be suitable for people with diabetes.
Vegan Diet: The standard recipe for Strawberry Acai does not include any animal products, making it suitable for vegans. However, if it is prepared with additions like milk or cream, it would not be vegan. Always specify your dietary preferences when ordering.
Lactose Intolerance: As long as no dairy products are added to the Strawberry Acai, it should be suitable for those with lactose intolerance.
High Blood Pressure: Strawberry Acai is not high in sodium, but it does contain sugars. If you are monitoring your sugar intake due to high blood pressure, keep this in mind.
