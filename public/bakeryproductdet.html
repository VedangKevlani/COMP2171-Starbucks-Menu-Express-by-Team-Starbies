Cinnamon Raisin Bagel

Product Details

Experience the comforting embrace of a New York-style bagel, generously thick and delightfully chewy. It is a symphony of flavors with sweet cinnamon swirls and plump raisins dancing through every bite. A timeless classic, reimagined.

Dietary Restrictions

Gluten Intolerance/Celiac Disease: The bread flour in the bagel contains gluten, which is not suitable for people with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.
Diabetes: The sugar in the bagel can affect blood sugar levels, so it may not be suitable for people with diabetes.
Vegan Diet: The recipe includes egg and honey, which are not suitable for vegans.
Egg Allergy: People with an egg allergy should avoid this bagel due to the presence of egg.
High Blood Pressure: The recipe may contain salt, which should be limited in people with high blood pressure.
